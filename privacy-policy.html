1. Introduction

Welcome to Littyverse: Galaxy Dash (“we”, “our”, “us”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile game and related services.

By installing or using the game, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

The core game does not require account creation for basic gameplay. If optional features are added in the future (such as leaderboards, cloud saves, or support contact forms), we may collect:

  • Username or display name;
  • Email address (optional);
  • Information you include when you contact us for support or feedback.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

To improve gameplay and performance, we may automatically collect certain non-personal information, such as:

  • Device model, operating system version, and language;
  • Gameplay statistics (for example, scores, levels reached, items collected);
  • Crash logs, error reports, and performance data;
  • Basic usage analytics (for example, how often the game is opened).

This information is used in an aggregated or anonymized way and is not used to personally identify you.

2.3 Third-Party Services

In some versions of the game, we may integrate third-party services such as:

  • Platform services (e.g., Google Play Games, Apple Game Center);
  • Analytics services (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Game Analytics);
  • Advertising networks (e.g., AdMob, Unity Ads), if ads are enabled.

These third parties may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing those policies for more details.

3. How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the game and related services;
  • Fix bugs, crashes, and technical issues;
  • Balance gameplay, difficulty, and in-game economy;
  • Protect the integrity of the game and reduce cheating or abuse;
  • Provide support if you contact us directly.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Advertising

Some versions of the game may display ads. When ads are enabled, advertising partners may use limited data (such as device identifiers, approximate location, and interaction with ads) to provide, measure, and improve the ads you see.

You can typically limit or reset ad identifiers and disable personalized ads in your device settings.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Gameplay data may be stored locally on your device and, if cloud features are enabled, on secure servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Our game is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or under the minimum age in your region) without verifiable parental consent.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so that we can investigate and remove the information if necessary.

Depending on your region, you may have certain rights regarding your data, such as the right to:

  • Request access to information we hold about you;
  • Request correction or deletion of your information;
  • Object to or restrict certain forms of processing;

You can also manage many privacy settings directly in your device (for example, ad tracking, analytics, or permissions).
