Community Guidelines

Last updated: November 2025

The Littyverse is about fun, creativity, and good vibes. These Community Guidelines help keep Littyverse: Galaxy Dash a safe and respectful space for everyone.

1. Be Respectful

  • No harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
  • No threats, stalking, or encouragement of self-harm or violence.
  • Respect different backgrounds, identities, and viewpoints.

2. Keep It Clean

  • No sexually explicit, extremely violent, or graphic content.
  • No posting or sharing illegal content.
  • Follow platform age ratings and content rules.

3. Fair Play

  • No cheating, hacking, exploiting bugs, or using unauthorized tools.
  • No impersonating developers, staff, or other players.
  • No selling or trading accounts, Virtual Goods, or services for real money unless explicitly allowed.

4. Privacy & Safety

  • Do not share personal information (addresses, phone numbers, passwords, financial details) of yourself or others.
  • Report suspicious or harmful behavior to us and the platform provider.

5. Enforcement

We may take actions including warnings, temporary suspensions, permanent bans, or removal of content if these Guidelines or the Terms of Service are violated.

6. Reporting

If you encounter behavior that violates these Guidelines, please contact us with as much detail as possible (screenshots, usernames, time of incident).

Contact: support@littyverse.com

These Guidelines work together with our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
