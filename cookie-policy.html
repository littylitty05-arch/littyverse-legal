Cookie Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that may be stored on your device when you visit a website or use certain online services. Similar technologies (such as SDKs, pixels, and local storage) may also be used in our apps.

2. How We Use Cookies

When you visit Littyverse legal pages or related web experiences, we may use cookies to:

  • Remember basic settings and preferences;
  • Understand how many people visit our pages and which sections they use;
  • Improve performance, security, and user experience.

3. Types of Cookies

  • Strictly necessary cookies – required for the site to function.
  • Performance / analytics cookies – help us understand usage and improve our services.
  • Functional cookies – remember preferences such as language.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party analytics or hosting providers that place their own cookies, subject to their privacy policies. We do not control these cookies directly.

5. Managing Cookies

Most browsers and devices allow you to manage or delete cookies in settings. If you disable some cookies, parts of our site may not function properly.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. The “Last updated” date at the top will indicate the latest version.
